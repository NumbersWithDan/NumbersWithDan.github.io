Academic Projects & Mathematical Explorations

Notes, problems, and insights from an academic journey

Numerical Analysis

My work in numerical analysis focuses on understanding and implementing computational methods for solving mathematical problems. This includes studying ancient approximation techniques like the Babylonian method for square roots, as well as modern numerical algorithms for differential equations, optimization, and linear algebra.

Real Analysis

Real Analysis provides the rigorous mathematical foundation for calculus and advanced mathematics. My studies in this field include understanding the completeness of real numbers, limits and continuity, differentiation and integration, and the fundamental theorems that underpin modern analysis. These concepts are essential for understanding the theoretical foundations of numerical methods and statistics.

Statistics & Probability

My work in statistics covers both theoretical foundations and practical applications. This includes probability theory, statistical inference, hypothesis testing, regression analysis, and experimental design. These mathematical tools are essential for data science and provide the theoretical underpinnings for machine learning and statistical modeling.
