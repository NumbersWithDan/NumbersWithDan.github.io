Numerical Analysis

Computational methods for solving mathematical problems

Overview

Numerical analysis focuses on developing and analyzing algorithms for solving mathematical problems using computers. This field bridges pure mathematics and practical computation, providing methods for approximating solutions to problems that may not have exact analytical solutions.
