NBA Data Analysis

Exploring basketball through data science and statistical analysis

Team Performance Analysis

I analyze NBA data to uncover patterns in team performance, strategic trends, and game outcomes. Using advanced statistical methods and machine learning, I explore topics like offensive and defensive efficiency, pace of play, and how different playing styles affect team success.

My analyses combine traditional basketball statistics with modern advanced metrics like Player Efficiency Rating (PER), True Shooting Percentage, and Win Shares to provide deeper insights into what makes teams successful in different situations.

Shot Selection & Efficiency

I study how teams and players optimize their shot selection to maximize scoring efficiency. This includes analyzing the value of different shot types, understanding how spacing affects shooting percentages, and identifying trends in how the game is evolving toward more efficient offensive strategies.

Lineup Analysis

Understanding how different player combinations perform together is crucial for optimal team construction. I analyze lineup data to identify which player combinations work best in different situations, how chemistry affects performance, and what makes certain lineups particularly effective or ineffective.

Game Flow & Momentum

I study how games develop over time, analyzing momentum shifts, scoring runs, and how teams respond to different game situations. This includes understanding the psychology of basketball and how statistical patterns can help predict and explain game outcomes.

Advanced Metrics Development

I work on developing new metrics and analytical frameworks to better understand the game. This includes creating composite efficiency ratings, analyzing player impact on team performance, and studying the evolution of player skills over time.

About My NBA Analytics

I combine my passion for basketball with data science to explore the game from new angles. My analyses focus on finding insights that traditional statistics might miss, using advanced analytical methods to better understand team and player performance.
