NBA Analytics & Sports Projects

Exploring basketball through data science and statistical analysis

NBA Data Analysis

I analyze NBA data to uncover patterns in player performance, team strategies, and game outcomes. Using advanced statistical methods and machine learning, I explore topics like player efficiency, shot selection, and team chemistry. My analyses combine traditional basketball statistics with modern advanced metrics to provide deeper insights into the game.

Player Statistics & Performance Metrics

I develop and analyze advanced player metrics that go beyond traditional box score statistics. This includes creating composite efficiency ratings, analyzing player impact on team performance, and studying the evolution of player skills over time. My work helps identify undervalued players and predict future performance trends.

Game Predictions & Salary Analysis

One of my featured projects involves using machine learning to predict NBA player salaries based on performance metrics. By analyzing historical data on player statistics, team success, and market conditions, I've developed models that can estimate player value and predict salary trends. This work combines sports analytics with economic modeling to understand the business side of basketball.

NBA Player Salary Predictions

Using machine learning algorithms, I analyze player performance data to predict NBA salaries. The model considers factors like traditional statistics, advanced metrics, team performance, and market conditions to estimate player value. This project demonstrates the intersection of sports analytics and business intelligence.
