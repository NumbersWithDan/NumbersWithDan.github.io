Interests & Miscellaneous

Beyond data and mathematics

Sports & Recreation

When I'm not diving into data analysis, I enjoy staying active and following sports. I'm an avid NBA fan and love analyzing player performance and team strategies. I also enjoy playing tennis and have recently taken up pickleball as I've gotten older. Occasionally, I attempt rock climbing, though I'm still working on building up my skills!

Urban Planning & Environmental Science

I'm fascinated by how data analysis can be applied to understand and improve urban environments. This interest led to my Dallas urban heat islands project, where I explored the relationship between urban development and climate impacts. I enjoy studying how cities can be designed more sustainably and equitably.

Technology & Innovation

I follow technology trends closely, particularly in the areas of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science tools. I'm always excited to learn about new programming languages, statistical software, and analytical techniques that can help solve complex problems.

Community & Learning

I believe in the power of community and continuous learning. I enjoy participating in data science meetups, academic discussions, and collaborative projects. Sharing knowledge and learning from others is one of the most rewarding aspects of working in this field.

Connecting Interests

What I find most exciting is how these different interests intersect. For example, using sports analytics to understand team dynamics, applying environmental data analysis to urban planning, or exploring how technology can make data science more accessible to broader communities.
