About Me

Get to know the person behind the numbers

Personal Life & Family

When I'm not diving deep into data or solving mathematical problems, I enjoy spending time with family and friends. I'm passionate about continuous learning and believe that curiosity is one of the most important traits for both personal growth and professional success. Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy following the NBA, playing tennis (and now pickleball as I've gotten older), and occasionally attempting rock climbing.

Professional Career

I'm currently pursuing my master's degree while working on various data science and analytics projects. My academic background combines mathematics, statistics, and computer science, providing a strong foundation for tackling complex analytical challenges. I'm passionate about using data to solve real-world problems and am always looking for new opportunities to apply my skills in meaningful ways.

Reading List & Learning

I'm an avid reader and lifelong learner. I enjoy books on mathematics, statistics, data science, and business analytics. Some of my favorite authors include Nate Silver, Edward Tufte, and various academic researchers in the field of statistical learning. I believe that continuous learning is essential for staying current in the rapidly evolving field of data science.

Career History

My journey in data science and analytics has been driven by a curiosity about how numbers can reveal hidden patterns and insights. I've worked on projects spanning various industries and domains, always focusing on applying rigorous analytical methods to solve practical problems. Connect with me on LinkedIn to learn more about my professional background.

Miscellaneous Interests

Beyond data and mathematics, I have a wide range of interests including sports analytics, urban planning, environmental science, and technology trends. I enjoy exploring how data analysis can be applied to understand and improve various aspects of society and the environment.
