Family & Personal Life

Get to know the person behind the numbers

Personal Life & Family

When I'm not diving deep into data or solving mathematical problems, I enjoy spending time with family and friends. I'm passionate about continuous learning and believe that curiosity is one of the most important traits for both personal growth and professional success. Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy following the NBA, playing tennis (and now pickleball as I've gotten older), and occasionally attempting rock climbing.

Family is incredibly important to me, and I value the support and encouragement they provide in all my endeavors. Whether it's discussing the latest data science trends over dinner or explaining why a particular statistical method is so elegant, I appreciate having people in my life who share my enthusiasm for learning and discovery.

Hobbies & Interests

Beyond mathematics and data science, I have a wide range of interests that keep me engaged and balanced. I'm an avid sports fan, particularly of basketball, and enjoy analyzing games from both a statistical and strategic perspective. I also enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and rock climbing, which provide a nice contrast to the more cerebral work I do during the day.

Learning & Growth

I believe that continuous learning is essential for both personal and professional development. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge, whether through formal education, online courses, or hands-on projects. I particularly enjoy exploring the intersection of different fields, such as how mathematical concepts apply to sports analytics or how statistical methods can be used to solve real-world problems.

Community Involvement

I believe in giving back to the community and sharing knowledge with others. I enjoy mentoring students who are interested in data science and mathematics, and I'm always happy to discuss career paths, academic challenges, or interesting problems with anyone who's curious about these fields.

About This Site

This website showcases my work in data science, mathematics, and sports analytics. It's a place where I share my projects, academic work, and thoughts on the intersection of data and real-world applications.
