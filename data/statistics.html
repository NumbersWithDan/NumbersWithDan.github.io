Statistical Analysis & Methods

Applying rigorous statistical methods to solve real-world problems

Hypothesis Testing & Inference

I apply rigorous statistical methods to solve real-world problems, from hypothesis testing and regression analysis to advanced multivariate techniques. My work includes experimental design, A/B testing, and statistical modeling to support business decisions and research questions.

This includes designing and analyzing experiments to test hypotheses about user behavior, product performance, and market trends. I use appropriate statistical tests and confidence intervals to ensure reliable conclusions from data.

Regression Analysis

I specialize in various forms of regression analysis, including linear, logistic, and polynomial regression models. These techniques help identify relationships between variables and make predictions about future outcomes based on historical data patterns.

Experimental Design

Proper experimental design is crucial for obtaining reliable results. I design and analyze controlled experiments, including randomized controlled trials, factorial designs, and quasi-experimental studies. This ensures that conclusions drawn from data are statistically valid and practically meaningful.

A/B Testing & Optimization

I design and analyze A/B tests to optimize user experiences, product features, and business processes. This includes determining appropriate sample sizes, setting up statistical tests, and interpreting results to make data-driven decisions about changes and improvements.

Multivariate Analysis

For complex datasets with multiple variables, I apply advanced multivariate techniques including principal component analysis, factor analysis, and cluster analysis. These methods help identify underlying patterns and relationships that might not be apparent from simple univariate analysis.

Statistical Modeling

I develop statistical models to understand complex relationships in data and make predictions about future outcomes. This includes time series analysis, survival analysis, and Bayesian statistical methods for situations where traditional frequentist approaches may not be appropriate.

About My Statistical Work

I believe that rigorous statistical methods are essential for making reliable conclusions from data. My approach combines theoretical understanding with practical application to solve real-world problems.
