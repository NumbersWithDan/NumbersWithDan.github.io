Data Science & Analytics Projects

Exploring insights through data analysis, statistics, and machine learning

Data Analysis Projects

My data analysis work focuses on extracting meaningful insights from complex datasets. I use Python, R, and SQL to clean, analyze, and visualize data, helping organizations make data-driven decisions. Key projects include urban heat island analysis, economic trend analysis, and customer behavior studies.

Statistical Analysis

I apply rigorous statistical methods to solve real-world problems, from hypothesis testing and regression analysis to advanced multivariate techniques. My work includes experimental design, A/B testing, and statistical modeling to support business decisions and research questions.

Machine Learning

I develop and implement machine learning models using Python and R, including supervised and unsupervised learning techniques. Projects range from predictive modeling and classification to clustering and recommendation systems. I focus on creating models that are both accurate and interpretable, with proper validation and testing procedures.

Dallas Urban Heat Islands Project

One of my featured projects analyzes temperature patterns across Dallas neighborhoods to understand urban heat island effects and their socioeconomic implications. This project combines geospatial analysis, statistical modeling, and data visualization to identify patterns and inform urban planning decisions.
